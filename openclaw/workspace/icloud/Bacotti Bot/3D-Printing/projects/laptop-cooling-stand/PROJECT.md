# Laptop Cooling Stand with iPhone Holder + Mouse Pad

**Status:** Idea / Research
**Created:** 2026-04-16
**Folder:** `projects/laptop-cooling-stand/`

## Concept
All-in-one desk station for Mike's desk:
- Laptop stand with passive or active cooling
- Integrated iPhone holder (visible during calls)
- Attached mouse pad surface

## Features
- Laptop rest area with angled surface for airflow
- iPhone holder (side or front mounted)
- Mouse pad area (silicone or textured print surface)
- Cable management channel
- Fan mount points (optional active cooling)

## Design Specs
- Fits 13"-16" laptops
- Phone holder accommodates various phone sizes
- Mouse pad surface: target ~25cm x 20cm
