# Working Puzzles

**Status:** Idea / Research
**Created:** 2026-04-16
**Folder:** `projects/working-puzzles/`

## Concept
Interlocking 3D printed pieces that actually move — teaches mechanical principles while being fun to assemble and play with.

## Puzzle Types
- **Gear puzzles** — interlocking gears that turn together, teach gear ratios
- **Hinged puzzles** — pieces connected by living hinges (PETG or TPU)
- **Sliding puzzles** — pieces that slide in printed tracks
- **Ball bearing mazes** — navigate a ball through a 3D maze
- **Interlocking robots** — pieces that snap together to form a figure

## Educational Value
- Mechanical advantage and gear ratios
- Spatial reasoning
- Assembly order thinking
- Tolerance and fit understanding
