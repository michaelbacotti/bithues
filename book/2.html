class="detail-title">The Richmond Cipher

by E. Maris

About this book

In Richmond, 1863, Mary listens as generals speak freely, certain no one below the stairs can understand. Every word she carries becomes a weapon in this tense spy thriller inspired by the real Elizabeth Van Lew network.

Who would enjoy this

Readers of Kristin Hannah, James McBride, and Anthony Doerr. Fans of Civil War fiction and espionage.
