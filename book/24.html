Rules of Survival

Fire, Hunger, and the First Name

by Jorak Veldt

About this book

Roughly one million years ago, a boy learns the band's rules—water order, ember law, watch—and pays for them in skin. Grounded in archaeological inference.

Who would enjoy this

Fans of deep-time settings and anthropologically grounded survival fiction.
