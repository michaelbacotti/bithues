The Dawn of Civilization

A Story of a Prehistoric Tribe's Struggle for Survival

by T. Stone

About this book

When a brutal winter kills his father, sixteen-year-old Koda watches Uluk take up the leader's staff. As bears, bandits, and a bronze-armed warlord close in, Koda and his friends fight to turn a wandering tribe into a walled village.

Who would enjoy this

Fans of gritty historical adventures, tribal survival stories, and early-civilization settings.
