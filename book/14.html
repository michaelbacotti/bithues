Otomí

A Historical Narrative of Land, Ritual, and Continuity

by E. J. Marín

About this book

Through the eyes of a young ritual apprentice, follow one drought-stricken year in an Otomí village, drawing on archaeology and cosmology to reconstruct ancient life.

Who would enjoy this

Bernard Cornwell fans and lovers of indigenous historical fiction.
