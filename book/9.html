The Shadow Within

A Practical Guide to Safe, Realistic Shadow Work in Everyday Life

by Elena Maris

About this book

Instead of treating reactions as defects, learn to treat them as information—and respond differently. A trauma-informed, Jungian approach to shadow work that emphasizes grounding and safety.

Who would enjoy this

People who know their patterns but struggle to change reactions. Jungian psychology fans.
