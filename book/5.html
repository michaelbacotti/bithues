Living with a Moving Planet

Deep Time, Human Adaptation, and a Positive Climate Future

by J. T. Hartley

About this book

Drawing on deep-time climate records and archaeology, this book shows how humans have adapted to changing baselines for tens of thousands of years—and what we can learn for living with a changing planet.

Who would enjoy this

Anyone tired of doom and denial who wants a grounded, hopeful way to think about climate.
