## Week Type 1 — Standard Training Week
*(No meet scheduled)*

### Monday Practice: Trail Run + Form Work
- 20–25 min trail run at easy conversational pace
- During run: 4 × 30-sec strides (pickup speed, then ease off)
- End with full stretch routine

### Tuesday Home Run
- 20–30 min easy run at very light effort
- No structured pace — just move
- Can be on trail, road, or neighborhood

### Wednesday Stretch & Strength
- 15–20 min:
  - 5 min dynamic stretching (leg swings, walking lunges, arm circles)
  - 10 min bodyweight: 15 squats, 10 lunges each leg, 20 calf raises, 15 hip bridges, 10 push-ups
  - 5 min foam rolling or static stretching

### Thursday Practice: Hill Repeats
- 5 min easy jog warm-up
- 6 × hill climb (40 sec hard / walk down 60 sec)
- 5 min easy cool-down + full stretch

### Friday Home Run
- 20 min easy run
- Keep it very light — tomorrow is a rest/easy day feeling

---

## Week Type 2 — Meet Week
*(Meet on Saturday: May 2, 5, 8, 12)*

### Monday Practice: Light Speed Prep
- 15 min easy trail run
- 4 × 100m strides at ~70% effort (good form, relaxed)
- No hard efforts — just wake up the fast-twitch

### Tuesday Home Run
- 20 min very easy
- Very light — just circulation

### Wednesday Stretch & Strength
- Same as standard, but take it easy
- Focus on hip flexors and calves (tighten up on race day if not stretched)

### Thursday Practice: Pre-Meet Shakeout
- 10–15 min easy jog
- 4 × 50m strides at 60% — stay loose
- Full stretch routine (spend extra time here)

### Friday Home Run (Race Prep)
- 15 min easy + 3 × 50m very gentle pick-ups
- Keep EVERYTHING feeling loose and fresh

### Saturday: 🏁 MEET DAY
- Arrive warm — jog 10 min before call to arms
- Team stretch together
- Race!
- Cool-down jog after your race (coach will remind you)

## Coach's Notes

**On effort:**
- Easy days should feel *easy* — you should be able to hold a conversation
- Hard days are hard — but we don't go to 100%. 80–85% max
- If something hurts, tell me right away — no pushing through pain

**What to bring to every practice:**
- Running shoes (trail shoes if you have them)
- Water bottle
- Weather-appropriate layers

**Rest days are NOT zero days:**
- Walk, stretch, move gently
- Rest means let your body recover
