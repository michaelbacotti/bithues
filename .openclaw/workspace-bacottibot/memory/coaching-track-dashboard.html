Coach's Rules

  • Easy days = EASY. You should be able to hold a full conversation. If you're gasping, slow down.
  • Hard days = structured. We go harder on Mon/Thu but never to 100%. 80–85% max.
  • Something hurts? Tell me immediately. No pushing through pain — that leads to more time off.
  • What to bring every practice: running shoes, water bottle, weather layers
  • Rest days are NOT zero. Walk, stretch, move gently — but don't train.
  • Thursday practice before a meet = shortest practice of the week. Fresh legs win races.
