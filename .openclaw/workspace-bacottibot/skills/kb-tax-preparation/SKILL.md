- "Tax", "taxes", "tax preparation", "filing", "1065", "1120", "990"

- K-1 distributions, estimated tax payments
- Wash-sale loss carryforward utilization

## When NOT to use

- For general entity overview (use kb-entity-overview)
- For accounting reconciliation (use kb-accounting-review)

## Outputs
