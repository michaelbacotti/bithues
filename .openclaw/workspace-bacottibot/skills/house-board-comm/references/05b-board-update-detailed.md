# Template: Board Update — Full Email Format

**Use when:** Sending a detailed board update via email or longer text about a new or urgent rental assistance application. For SMS-short updates use `05-board-update.md`.

**Mike's preferred format (proven, clean):**

```
Hi everyone,
I have an update:

Applicant: Ms. {{first_name}} {{last_name}} — {{city}}, NV
Situation: {{situation_summary — lease status, amount owed, key hardship facts, why they need help}}
Landlord: {{landlord_name}}, {{company}}. Confirmed active lease, willing to work with tenant. Verified balance of ${{verified_balance}}.

I propose we pay the full ${{amount}} by mailed check directly to {{landlord_name}} at {{company}}. Our Chase account has over ${{account_balance}} at this time. This would leave roughly ${{remaining_balance}}.

Please let me know of any objections. If I don't hear from anyone I'll proceed.
```

**Key elements of this format:**
- Brief, conversational opener
- Clear situation summary — one paragraph, no fluff
- Landlord confirmation — one line
- Specific ask — exact amount, payment method, payee
- Financial context — account balance and remainder
- Simple call to action — objections or proceed

**What to include:**
- Verified (not estimated) balance
- Landlord name and confirmation they are cooperative
- Exact payment method and payee
- Available account balance for context

**What NOT to include:**
- SSN, DOB, full applicant address
- Lease terms beyond basic validity
- Bank routing or account numbers

**⚠️ Accuracy requirement:**
Write only what is directly stated in the applicant or landlord's own words. Do not infer, assume, or fill gaps with speculation. If a detail is not in the record, leave it out or note it as "unknown."
