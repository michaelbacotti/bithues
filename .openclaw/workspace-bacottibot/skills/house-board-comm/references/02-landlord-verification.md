# Template: Landlord Verification Request

*Sent when verifying a rental assistance applicant — confirms tenancy, balance, and payment process before board approval.*

---

**Suggested subject:** Rent Payment Inquiry - Apt {{unit_number}}

**From:** Michael Bacotti \<michaelbacotti@houseinc501c3.com\>
*(Use this address — michaelbacotti@houseinc501c3.com — not the generic info@ address)*

---

Dear {{landlord_company_name}}{{landlord_first_name_filter: or %s}},

My name is Michael Bacotti with HOUSE, Inc., a 501c3 nonprofit organization. We work with individuals and families at risk of homelessness to provide housing assistance.

We have been contacted by {{applicant_first_name}} {{applicant_last_name}}, a tenant at {{property_address}}, who has requested rental assistance from our organization. We are considering providing financial assistance to cover past-due rent, but before proceeding I would like to verify a few details:

Is {{applicant_first_name}} {{applicant_last_name}} currently a tenant in good standing (lease valid, no active eviction, no major violations)?

What is the exact amount currently owed on their account?

Can past-due rent be paid by mailed check or electronically? Do you have a website portal?

Please note that if we approve assistance, **payment would be made directly to you** rather than to the tenant.

I appreciate your time and look forward to hearing back from you.

Sincerely,
Michael Bacotti
HOUSE, Inc.

---

## Placeholders

| Placeholder | Description |
|---|---|
| `{{landlord_company_name}}` | Property management company or landlord name (e.g. "Ashton Park Apartments") |
| `{{landlord_first_name}}` | First name of individual landlord/property manager (optional — omit if unknown) |
| `{{applicant_first_name}}` | Applicant's first name |
| `{{applicant_last_name}}` | Applicant's last name |
| `{{property_address}}` | Full property address with unit number |
| `{{unit_number}}` | Unit number (for email subject line) |

## When to use this template

- Board has voted to approve assistance pending landlord verification
- Or: when a quick landlord outreach may buy time for an urgent case (eviction imminent)
- You need to confirm: (a) tenant is in good standing, (b) exact amount owed, (c) how to pay by check or electronically

## Tips

- Use a personalized greeting when you have a contact name: "Dear Ashton Park Apartments or Lilly,"
- Use the michaelbacotti@houseinc501c3.com sender address — more personal than the generic info@ address
- Keep the signature block short — full contact info in the body is not always necessary for initial outreach
- Allow 2-3 business days before following up if no response; call if urgent
