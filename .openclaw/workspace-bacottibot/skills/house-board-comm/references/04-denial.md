# Template: Denial

**Use when:** Declining a rental assistance application after board review. Must be compassionate, clear, and encouraging.

**Rules encoded:**
- Does NOT blame the applicant
- Explains limited resources without making the applicant feel unworthy
- Invites reapplication if circumstances change
- Warm, encouraging tone
- No sensitive info in email

---

**Subject:** Update on Your Rental Assistance Application

Dear {{applicant_name}},

Thank you for applying to HOUSE Inc. and for sharing your situation with us. We want you to know that your application received careful attention and genuine consideration.

After thoughtful review by our board, we are unfortunately **unable to approve rental assistance at this time**. This decision reflects the limits of our current resources rather than any judgment of your need or your circumstances.

**We want to leave the door open for you:**

If your situation changes — for example, if your income increases, your expenses decrease, or our funding picture improves — we encourage you to apply again. HOUSE Inc. is here as an ongoing resource for families in our community, and we never want a denial today to be mistaken for a permanent "no."

In the meantime, here are a few resources that may be helpful while you navigate this period:

- **Nevada 2-1-1** — Dial 2-1-1 (or visit nevada211.org) for local housing and utility assistance referrals
- **Southern Nevada Regional Housing Authority** — snrha.org | 702-486-8400
- **Catholic Charities of Southern Nevada** — ccsnn.org | 702-385-0159

If you have questions about your application or would like to speak with someone on our board, please call **702-871-8535** or email **donations@houseinc501c3.com**.

We know this is not the outcome you were hoping for, and we sincerely wish you and your family strength during this time.
