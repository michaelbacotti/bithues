# Template: Approval + Landlord Verification

**Use when:** The board has approved rental assistance and you need to (1) notify the applicant and (2) ask the landlord to confirm tenancy and the amount owed.

**Rules encoded:**
- Commits to funding (board has approved)
- Requests landlord contact info + current bill/statement
- Does NOT reveal the approved amount to the landlord in this email
- Pays landlord directly

---
