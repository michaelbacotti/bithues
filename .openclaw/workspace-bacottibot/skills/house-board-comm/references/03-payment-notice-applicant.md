# Template: Payment Notice to Applicant

*Sent after board approval — to notify the applicant that payment has been sent to their landlord.*

---

**Suggested subject:** Update on Your Request — HOUSE, Inc.

**From:** Michael Bacotti \<michaelbacotti@houseinc501c3.com\>

---

Dear {{applicant_first_name}},

I wanted to let you know that I have scheduled a payment of ${{amount}} on your behalf to {{landlord_name}} to help cover past-due rent. The payment has been sent as a check and is scheduled to be delivered by {{delivery_date}}.

I truly hope this helps ease your situation and provides some support during a difficult time. I am hoping for the best as you work through your finances.

We are a very small charity, and our mission is to help people facing situations like this. Please consider this assistance a grant. At the same time, if you are ever in a position to repay any portion of it in the future, those funds would go directly toward helping others who are going through similar hardship.

We are only able to help a limited number of people, but we do what we can. I sincerely hope this payment brings some relief and helps move things in a better direction.

Wishing you the best,
Michael Bacotti

---

## Placeholders

| Placeholder | Description |
|---|---|
| `{{applicant_first_name}}` | Applicant's first name |
| `{{amount}}` | Exact payment amount |
| `{{landlord_name}}` | Landlord or property name |
| `{{delivery_date}}` | Expected delivery date |

## When to use this template

- After board approves assistance and payment has been mailed
- Always include the grant language: "please consider this assistance a grant"
- Mention the repayment-as-donation option as optional — not required

## Tips

- Keep warm but not effusive
- Be honest about being a small charity
- The repayment mention is optional — some cases may not need it
