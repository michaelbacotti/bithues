# Template: Board Update

**Use when:** Drafting a short summary of a new rental assistance application for Mike to text or email to HOUSE Inc. board members.

**Rules encoded:**
- Must be ≤ 300 characters (SMS-friendly)
- Includes only essential, non-sensitive summary info
- NO SSN, bank info, DOB, or full address of applicant
- Designed for quick board review / vote

---

**Template (fill in the fields below):**

> HOUSE Inc. Board Update: New application received — **{{applicant_first_name}} D.** (last initial only), {{city}}, NV. Rent amount owed: **${{amount}}**. Landlord: {{landlord_name}}. Applicant can contribute: **{{contribution_amount}}**. {{can_contribute_yes_no}}. Awaiting landlord verification docs. Recommended action: **{{APPROVE / DENY / HOLD}}**. — Please reply APPROVE, DENY, or HOLD by {{date}}.

---

**Character count guidance:** The template above runs approximately 240–280 characters with typical filled values. Avoid adding extra details. If the requested amount exceeds available board funds, note that in the action field (e.g., "APPROVE up to $X — hold remainder").

**What to include:**
- Applicant first name + last initial
- City (for reference)
- Amount owed
- Landlord name
- Applicant's stated contribution
- Current status
- Requested board action with deadline

**What NOT to include:**
- Full applicant name
- SSN, DOB, or full address
- Bank account details
- Lease terms or rental duration
