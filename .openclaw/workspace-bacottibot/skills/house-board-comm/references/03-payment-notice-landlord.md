# Template: Payment Notice to Landlord

*Sent after board approval — to notify the landlord that a payment is being sent.*

---

**Suggested subject:** Payment Scheduled — {{applicant_name}}, Unit {{unit_number}}

**From:** Michael Bacotti \<michaelbacotti@houseinc501c3.com\>

---

Dear {{landlord_first_name_or_company}},

I wanted to let you know that I have scheduled a payment of ${{amount}} to {{property_name}} on behalf of {{applicant_name}} to help cover past-due rent. The payment has been funded and is being sent by check, with delivery scheduled for {{delivery_date}}.

We hope this payment helps stabilize the matter and provides some meaningful relief.

We are a very small charity, and we do our best to help people facing urgent housing hardship when we are able. I am hopeful this payment will help move things in a better direction for everyone involved.

Thank you for your time.

Sincerely,
Michael Bacotti
houseinc501c3.org

---

## Placeholders

| Placeholder | Description |
|---|---|
| `{{landlord_first_name_or_company}}` | Landlord's name or property company |
| `{{amount}}` | Exact payment amount |
| `{{property_name}}` | Property name (e.g. "Ashton Park Apartments") |
| `{{applicant_name}}` | Applicant's full name |
| `{{unit_number}}` | Unit number |
| `{{delivery_date}}` | Expected delivery date |

## When to use this template

- After board approves assistance and payment has been mailed
- Send to landlord to confirm payment is on the way and discourage eviction filing
