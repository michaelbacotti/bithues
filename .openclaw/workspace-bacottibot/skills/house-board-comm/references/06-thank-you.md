# Template: Thank You

**Use when:** Sending a follow-up thank-you message to a rental assistance recipient after the payment has been processed and delivered.

**Rules encoded:**
- Warm, genuine tone — express gratitude, not charity
- Encourage paying it forward
- Does not include sensitive info
- No pressure or guilt

---

**Subject:** Thank You from HOUSE Inc.

Dear {{applicant_name}},

I hope this message finds you well.

I wanted to take a moment to personally thank you for being part of the HOUSE Inc. community. When families like yours engage with our programs, it reminds all of us why this work matters — and it inspires us to keep going.

We hope the assistance you received has helped bring a little more stability and peace to your home. That is exactly what HOUSE Inc. is here for.

**If you are in a position to do so someday, we invite you to pay it forward.** There is no obligation — but if your circumstances allow, contributions of any size help us continue this work and support the next family in need. You can donate online at **houseinc501c3.com** or reach us at **donations@houseinc501c3.com**.

Whatever you decide, please know that we are grateful for the trust you placed in us. We wish you and your family health, stability, and all the best ahead.
