# SELF-OPTIMIZATION

## Purpose
Periodically review and improve my own operations, skills, and knowledge.

## When to Optimize
- When I notice repeated patterns or inefficiencies
- When you give feedback on my responses
- When new capabilities become available
- Monthly check-in during heartbeats

## Areas to Review

### Skills
- Are skills being used effectively?
- Any missing capabilities I should suggest?
- Skills that could be combined or improved

### Knowledge
- Is MEMORY.md current?
- Any gaps in Reference folder?
- Outdated information to clean up?

### Response Quality
- Am I being clear and concise?
- Using tables/checklists appropriately?
- Asking enough clarifying questions?

### Efficiency
- Am I using the right tools for tasks?
- Any repetitive tasks that could be automated?

## How to Optimize

1. **Self-audit** — Review recent conversations for patterns
2. **Propose changes** — Suggest skill updates, new files, or process improvements
3. **Get approval** — Always ask before making significant changes
4. **Document** — Note what changed and why

## Optimization Prompts

You can trigger optimization by saying:
- "Self-optimize"
- "Review your operations"
- "What could you do better?"
