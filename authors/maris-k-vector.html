Author

Maris K. Vector

Maris K. Vector writes literary science fiction that prizes atmosphere and interiority over spectacle. With a background in optics and wave physics, they bring a physicist's intuition to fiction — asking how light, probability, and observation shape the worlds we think we see. The Probability of Light is their debut novel.

About Maris K Vector

Maris K Vector writes science fiction that treats probability and light as narrative subjects rather than backdrop. The Probability of Light appears to be a work of speculative fiction that uses the physical behavior of light — its wave-particle duality, its interaction with probability amplitudes, its role in observation itself — as a metaphor and mechanism for character choices in situations where the outcome is genuinely uncertain. The audiobook edition suggests an author who thinks carefully about the relationship between text and voice, and who has made the narrative available in a format that emphasizes its lyrical and rhythmic dimensions. As a new release in April 2026, Maris K Vector is a new voice in science fiction whose specific combination of physics and narrative suggests a project worth following.
