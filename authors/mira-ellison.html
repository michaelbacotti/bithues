Mira Ellison

Science Fiction

Mira Ellison writes speculative fiction at the intersection of contact linguistics and first-contact protocol. Drawing on a background in comparative literature and SETI-adjacent research, her work explores what it might actually mean to hear from—and talk back to—another civilization. She lives in the Pacific Northwest and is currently working on a series examining communication beyond the binary of language.
