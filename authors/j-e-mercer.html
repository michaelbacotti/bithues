About J. E. Mercer

J. E. Mercer writes nonfiction that takes on the largest questions in contemporary style: life, consciousness, the universe, and what meaning is possible within them. The Perfection Cycle is currently available free in the Kindle Store and approaches its subject through the lens of cycles — natural, cosmic, personal — and argues that the human drive toward perfection is itself a cycical rather than linear process. Mercer writes with a philosophical directness that does not require prior familiarity with academic philosophy, yet treats the reader as capable of following a real argument. The free price point reflects an author more interested in readers than revenue — which is its own kind of statement about what writing is for.
