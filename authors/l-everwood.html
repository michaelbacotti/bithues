About L Everwood

L Everwood writes speculative fiction in the tradition of mid-century science fiction — fiction where the boundary between the physical and the metaphysical is genuinely uncertain rather than dramatically staged. Echoes of Transcendence follows seekers on a journey where the rules of reality are松动 and the destination is both external and internal. Everwood has a particular gift for rendering the phenomenology of altered perception: what it actually feels like to notice that the world has different rules than you assumed. The prose is controlled and precise, and the plot builds toward revelation in the classical science fiction mode — the reader understands more as the protagonist understands more, and neither fully understands the world they have entered by the end.
