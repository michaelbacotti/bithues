About Evelyn Carter

Evelyn Carter writes travel and cultural guides that refuse to be mere logistics documents. Discovering Washington DC is a comprehensive guide that moves confidently beyond the monuments to explore the lived city: Georgetown's cobblestones, the city's jazz history, the half-smoke as a cultural artifact, the neighborhoods that tourists pass through on their way to the Mall. Carter writes with the specificity of someone who has spent real time in a city — not the generalized enthusiasm of a visitor who has done a well-reviewed walking tour, but the granular affection of someone who knows which restaurant has the best window view and which museum courtyard is quietest on a Tuesday afternoon. For visitors planning a DC trip who want something more than a list of famous buildings, this guide offers a genuine alternative perspective on a city that rewards attention.
