Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: April 2026

Bithues Reading Lab is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. This affiliate advertising program is designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising commissions by linking to Amazon.

How It Works

When you click a link on Bithues Reading Lab and make a purchase on Amazon — not just the book you clicked on, but anything in your cart during that session — we may earn a small commission from that qualifying sale. This comes at no additional cost to you. The prices and availability you see on Amazon are exactly as they would be without our links; we have no ability to affect your purchase price.

Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships do not influence our reviews or recommendations. We cover books because we believe they are worth reading — not because they offer higher commission rates or because an author or publisher has a commercial relationship with us. Our editorial process is described in our Editorial Policy. Books we recommend are recommended because our reviewers found them genuinely worthwhile. We do not accept payment to review, promote, or feature any title.

FTC Compliance

We comply with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines regarding affiliate relationships and endorsements. This disclosure appears clearly on this page and is linked from the footer of every page on this site. We believe transparency is essential to maintaining the trust of our readers.

Questions?

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or anything else on the site, please get in touch. We are happy to provide additional detail about how the site is funded and how we maintain editorial independence.
