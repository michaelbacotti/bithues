What we look for

  • Strong ideas. Books that have something real to say — we skip fluffy fiction without substance.
  • Good writing. We're not grammar police, but the prose should be polished enough not to distract.
  • Genre fit. We focus on sci-fi, fantasy, self-help, nonfiction, and literary fiction. We occasionally cover thrillers, romance, and horror.
  • Independence welcomed. We love indie authors. About half our reviews are indie-published books.

What we don't review

  • Poetry collections (we make exceptions for narrative poetry)
  • Children's books under 8 years old
  • Textbooks or academic works
  • Short stories / novellas under 20,000 words
