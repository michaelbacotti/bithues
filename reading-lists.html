Curated Reading Lists

Themed pathways through the books we've reviewed. Save yourself hours of searching.

Self-Help

Books That Actually Change How You Think

Five self-help books that go beyond generic advice — each one has a specific, testable idea worth applying.

  • The Power of Changing Your Mind — intellectual humility as a competitive edge
  • The Shadow Within — treating your reactions as information
  • Time Investing — how to actually prioritize yourself
  • Mindful Memory — memory improvement through mindfulness
Sci-Fi

Sci-Fi for People Who Want Ideas, Not Just Action

Hard sci-fi and speculative fiction that will make you think differently about humanity, technology, and the universe.

  • The Confluence Doctrine — a world where suffering has been engineered out
  • Red Horizon: Lunar Launch — the first Mars colony launch goes wrong
  • Resonance Drift — first contact that isn't what we expected
  • Symbiont Bloom — aliens who aren't hostile, they're symbiotic
Fantasy

Fantasy That Builds Entire Worlds

Epic and urban fantasy with meticulous world-building — for readers who want to be fully immersed.

  • Echoes of Aetheris — four heroes, forgotten magic, a looming war
  • Otomí — a Mexico City bookstore holding doors between worlds
  • The Burning Song — a mute girl who hears the songs of stars
Nonfiction

Nonfiction That Makes You Smarter

Science, history, and philosophy books that surface genuinely new ideas — not just repackaged common wisdom.

  • Living with a Moving Planet — how humans adapted to changing baselines
  • Physics of Insight — how physics shapes our understanding of consciousness
  • The Physics of Time — what time actually is, and whether it can be reversed
Spiritual

What Happens When We Die? Books That Take It Seriously

Three very different approaches to the question of death and what might come after — from scientific to spiritual.

  • Beyond the Veil — a careful tour of evidence around death and near-death
  • Veiled Presence — self-discovery through intuition and awareness
Thriller

Fast-Paced Thrillers with Real Stakes

If you want a book you can't put down, these deliver — without sacrificing depth of character.

  • Rules of Survival — teenagers fight to the death on live TV
  • Blood Ember — a vampire detective uncovers a murder
