Living with a Moving Planet

by J. T. Hartley

A hopeful, practical guide to adapting to climate change through personal resilience and systemic thinking.

Key Takeaways

  • Balances alarm with actionable solutions
  • Emphasizes individual agency without ignoring systemic issues
  • Historical perspective on climate adaptation is eye-opening
  • Practical checklists for household and community resilience
Who should read this: Anyone feeling climate anxiety who wants constructive outlets for action.
Verdict: The most balanced climate book I've read — neither denial nor doom.
