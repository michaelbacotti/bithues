Red Horizon: Lunar Launch

by M. A. Hale

A near-future lunar colony faces sabotage and political intrigue in this fast-paced sci-fi thriller.

Key Takeaways

  • Technical details about lunar living feel grounded and plausible
  • The pacing never lets up — each chapter ends on a hook
  • Political dynamics between Earth and the colony feel prescient
  • Character arcs are satisfying even in a shorter format
Who should read this: Sci-fi fans who enjoy near-future realism with high stakes.
Verdict: Tight, engaging novella that delivers big ideas in a compact package.
