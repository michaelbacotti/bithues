Veiled Presence

by E. C. Stroud

In Veiled Presence: Questions on Earth's Silent Neighbors, researcher E. C. Stroud walks through the modern UAP/UFO record—not to proclaim a grand theory, but to map what can actually be supported by evidence. Organized around six questions: Who are they? Why are they here? What do they look like? How do they act? Where are they? What happens next?

Key Takeaways

  • Bridges psychology and spirituality in an accessible way
  • Practical exercises for developing intuition
  • Grounded in neuroscience without being dry
  • Good for readers new to mindfulness
Who should read this: Anyone curious about intuition but skeptical of woo-woo.
Verdict: A useful bridge between science and inner knowing.
