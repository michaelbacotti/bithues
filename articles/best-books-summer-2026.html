Best Books for Summer 2026

Your ultimate summer reading list — from beach reads to mind-expanding adventures

Summer reading means something different to everyone. For some, it's beach novels by the pool. For others, it's finally tackling that stack of ambitious reads. Either way, we've got you covered.

This list covers every summer reading mood — light escapes, big adventures, and books that expand your mind while you lounge in the sun.

For the Beach Bum: Light & Fun

Sometimes summer reading just needs to be enjoyable. These books deliver:

by Michael Jr.

Join Little Mike and his friends as they build an awe-inspiring sand castle. Perfect for reading with kids at the beach.

Your Summer Reading Challenge

Here's a simple challenge for summer 2026:

  • June: Finish one book from the "Light & Fun" list
  • July: Read one "Adventure" or "Mind-Expander"
  • August: Try something from the "Self-Improver" list

That's just 3 books for the whole summer. Totally doable — and you'll finish smarter, wiser, and more relaxed.
