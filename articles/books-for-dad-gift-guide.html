Best Books for Dad

Thoughtful gift ideas for every type of dad

Father's Day, Christmas, or just because — finding the right book for Dad can be tricky. Does he read at all? Does he prefer fiction or nonfiction? Is he into sci-fi or something more practical?

We've curated this gift guide based on the books we've reviewed. Every pick is one we genuinely recommend.

by M. A. Hale

A grounded, hopeful space colony story. Perfect for dads who loved The Martian but want something with more heart.
